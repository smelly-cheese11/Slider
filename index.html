DON'T STARVE TOGETHER:
SURVIVAL GAMES
Don't Starve Together is a standalone multiplayer expansion of the wilderness survival game, Don't Starve. It includes Reign of Giants, which adds new characters, seasons, creatures, biomes, and challenges to the game. The game takes place in a strange and unexplored world full of strange creatures, dangers, and surprises. Players must gather resources to craft items and structures that match their survival style.

MINECRAFT:
SURVIVAL GAMES
Minecraft is a 3D sandbox adventure game where players can interact with a fully customizable three-dimensional world made of blocks and entities. It was first made by Markus "Notch" Persson and sold to Microsoft in 2014 for 2.5 billion American dollars. Over 238 million copies of the game have been sold, making it the best-selling video game of all time. Minecraft encourages creativity, problem-solving, and collaboration

TERRARIA:
SANDBOX GAMES
Terraria is a 2011 action-adventure sandbox game developed by Re-Logic. The game was first released for Windows and has since been ported to other PC and console platforms. The game features exploration, crafting, building, painting, and combat with a variety of creatures in a procedurally generated 2D world.
